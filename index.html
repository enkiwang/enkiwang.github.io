Biography

Dr. Yongwei Wang is a Research Fellow on Artificial Intelligence at Nanyang Technological University (Supervisor: Prof. Chunyan Miao) in Singapore. He received his PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of British Columbia in 2021 (Supervisors: Prof. Z. Jane Wang and Prof. Rabab Ward), and his M.Sc and B.Eng (Honor) degrees from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Supervisor: Prof. Yixin Yang) in 2017 and 2014, respectively. He was also a visiting student at Delft University of Technology from Sept. 2013 to Mar. 2014 (Supervisor: Prof. Geert Leus).
