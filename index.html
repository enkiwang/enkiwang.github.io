Biography

Yongwei Wang is a ZJU100 Young Professor in Prof. Fei Wu's team, working on Safe, Efficient, and Generalizable AI at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou / Shanghai, China. Previously, he was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University working with Prof. Chunyan Miao's group from 2021 to 2023 in Singapore. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of British Columbia in 2021 (Advisors: Prof. Z. Jane Wang and Prof. Rabab Ward), and his M.Sc and B.Eng (Honors College) degrees from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Advisor: Prof. Yixin Yang) in 2017 and 2014, respectively. He was also a visiting student at Delft University of Technology from Sept. 2013 to Mar. 2014 (Advisor: Prof. Geert Leus).

Our team also collaborates closely with Baidu, Shanghai AI Lab, iFLYTEK, Alibaba, etc.

We are looking for **self-motivated** research interns/M.Sc/PhD students to join us!
